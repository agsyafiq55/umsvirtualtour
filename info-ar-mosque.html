How to Use

To experience the AR Mosque, you will need to use a Hiro Marker to display the 3D model of the UMS Mosque. Follow the instructions below carefully to enjoy the AR experience.

  1. Prepare two devices:
    • Device 1: Use this device's camera to scan the Hiro Marker.
    • Device 2: Use this device to display the Hiro Marker image.
  2. Download or open the Hiro Marker image (shown below) on Device 2.
  3. Use Device 1 to access the AR Mosque page and point the camera at the Hiro Marker displayed on Device 2.
  4. Once the Hiro Marker is scanned, the 3D model of the UMS Mosque will appear on your screen!

Click the button below when you're ready to start the AR Mosque experience.
